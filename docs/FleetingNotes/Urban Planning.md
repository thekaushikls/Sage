# Urban Planning
Created : 14-05-2023 09:15

Every high income area, needs to have a low income area nearby.  
  
Anna Nagar has Aminjikarai,  
Mumbai has Dharavi,  
New York has Brooklyn.  
  
While one group needs a source of income, the other need a source of man power.  
  
Now plug-in automation that only one group can afford. What happens to the other group? High-income group can change employees, use machines, etc.  
  
Should the weak be more resilient? Should they be able to switch skills faster? Does that mean most skilled people come from a low-income background?
