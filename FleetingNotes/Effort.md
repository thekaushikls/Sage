# Effort
Created : 11-04-2023 17:28

*Just enough to satisfy vs Enough to be satisfied* - the conflict to calculate how much effort is to be put into a certain task.

At work - if you are attached - you will aim for personal satisfaction.
This is also a luxury to expect at all times, as most timelines are short.
But it is also hard to switch to type 1, if you are accustomed to perfecting things.
