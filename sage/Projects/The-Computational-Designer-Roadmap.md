## About

This page is a documentation of my journey in becoming (calling myself) a Computational Designer, and sometimes a Computational Design Specialist - if I am feeling a bit special.

I aim to collaborate with other such individuals, to create a pictorial representation of this roadmap at [roadmap.sh](https://roadmap.sh/r?id=65335024b5d7a4eb01ee19cb). If this interests you, feel free to [connect](https://www.linkedin.com/in/thekaushikls).

## Introduction

When does one call themselves a Computational Designer? Is it when they master Rhino and Grasshopper? Or is it when they learn a little bit of Python and C#? Or are we completely off the mark, and the title reflects more on the confidence of the individual to take up certain kind of tasks?

To answer these questions, we first have to understand what [[../✏️ Notes/Computational -Design]] means.
