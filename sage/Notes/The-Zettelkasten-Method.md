## About

> A Zettelkasten Is a Personal Tool for Thinking and Writing[^1]

The Zettelkasten method is a tool to organize thoughts, ideas, references, and information in general, to create a well connected source of truth.

Usage of the Zettelkasten method usually falls into the domains of

1. Research
2. Personal Knowledge Management (PKM)
3. Reading and Writing
