## About

The four main types of modeling are
1. [Boundary-Representation-Modeling](../../Notes/Boundary-Representation-Modeling.md)
2. Polygonal (Mesh) Modeling
3. [Constructive-Solid-Geometry-Modeling](Constructive-Solid-Geometry-Modeling.md)
4. [Voxel-Modeling](Voxel-Modeling.md)

Hybrid Modeling leverages the advantage of each of these four modeling techniques, and use them in appropriate areas.
