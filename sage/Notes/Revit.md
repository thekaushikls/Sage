## Elements

1. Model Elements: Doors, Windows, etc.
2. Datum Elements: Grids, Levels, etc.
3. View Specific Elements: Tags, Texts, Dimensions, etc.

Elements are organized into groups called categories based on functionality.

## Category

Categories are further organized into Families

## Family

 Columns can be grouped based on type: Round, Rectangular, etc.
